Concise was built with my own years of experience as a web designer and developer, and my own ideals when it comes to what makes a great front-end framework. However, Concise would not be what it is today if it weren't for some amazing people that are releasing articles, videos, and resources that make people like myself a better designer and developer every single day.

So, without any further ado, I present you a list in no particular order, which outlines all of the resources and websites that had a great impact on Concise as a framework. If you are the creator of one of these pieces of content, consider this my heartfelt thank-you for your contribution and hard work.
