Nested Dropdowns

Nested dropdowns can be used to display and hide nested <ul> or <ol> tags.

Inside of the .nav-dropdown class, nested <ul> or <ol> lists are hidden by default. Upon hovering over a list item with a nested <ul> or <ol>, they are displayed.

Note: Concise only includes styling for up to three levels of nested lists. If more levels are added, additional styling will be required.
