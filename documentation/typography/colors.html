Colors

Nobody constantly wants black text on a black background, do they? Of course not. That's why Concise comes with various classes that can be used to change the color of text.

Preprocessor Note: You can change the $type-colors map in the _variables.scss file to adjust any of the base colors.

Note: I have given the white text a black background just for the purpose of demonstration.
